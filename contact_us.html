Frequently Asked Questions

1. What products do you offer?

We offer a diverse range of handcrafted furniture including sofas, tables, chairs, and more.

2. How can I place an order?

You can place an order through our website by selecting your desired items and completing the checkout process.

3. Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we offer international shipping. Shipping rates and delivery times may vary based on your location.

4. Can I customize the color and finish of the furniture?

Absolutely! We provide a range of customization options, including color, finish, and material choices.

5. What materials are used in your furniture?

Our furniture is crafted using high-quality and responsibly sourced materials, such as solid wood and premium upholstery fabrics.

6. Is assembly required for the furniture?

Most of our furniture comes pre-assembled. Any additional assembly required will be minimal, and instructions will be provided.

7. Do you offer a warranty on your products?

Yes, we provide a warranty on our products. Details of the warranty period and coverage can be found in our warranty policy.

8. How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track the status of your shipment.

9. What is your return policy?

We have a hassle-free return policy. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days for a refund or exchange.

10. Are your products environmentally friendly?

Yes, we prioritize sustainability. Our materials are sourced responsibly, and we are committed to reducing our environmental footprint.

11. Can I order a custom piece of furniture not listed on your website?

Certainly! Contact our customer support, and we'll be happy to discuss creating a custom piece tailored to your specifications.

12. How can I get in touch with your customer support?

You can reach our customer support team via email at support@artisanfurniture.com or by using the contact form on our website.

13. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment methods. You can choose your preferred payment option during checkout.

14. Do you offer gift cards?

Yes, we offer gift cards of various denominations. They make for perfect gifts for friends and family.

15. Can I change or cancel my order after it's placed?

Once an order is placed, changes may be possible within a short period. Please contact our customer support as soon as possible for assistance.

16. Are your furniture pieces easy to clean and maintain?

Yes, our furniture is designed for easy maintenance. Cleaning instructions are provided with each product.

17. Do you have a physical showroom where I can see the furniture in person?

Currently, we operate exclusively online. However, we provide detailed product descriptions and images to help you make an informed decision.

18. What is your lead time for custom orders?

The lead time for custom orders varies depending on the complexity and volume. Our team will provide you with an estimated timeline upon order confirmation.

19. Can I purchase replacement parts for your furniture?

Yes, we offer replacement parts for our furniture. Contact our customer support with the specific part you need.

20. Are discounts or promotions available for bulk orders?

Yes, we offer discounts for bulk or corporate orders. Contact our sales team to discuss your requirements and receive a customized quote.
